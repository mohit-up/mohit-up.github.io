Research

My primary area of focus is Computer Architecture. Currently, I am looking into accelerator design for deep learning applicati ons with focus on neural networks training and inference. I am also interested in embedded systems, CPU workload analysis and heterogeneous system design. During my undergraduate studies, I also worked on fault tolerance in network-on-chips.
During my undergraduate at BITS Pilani, I also worked on routing in Network-on-Chips under Dr. Soumya J.

Here are some of the previous projects that I have worked on:

Energy Efficient Mapping of Computer Vision Applications on Mobile Embedded Processing Platforms

Worked on the mapping of the KLT Object Tracking Algorithm on Heterogeneous Platforms on the Odroid XU-4 board by using parallel programming using OpenCL to improve the run time of the application

Reconfigurable Fault Tolerant Network-on-Chips

Did a study on different kinds of router and network architectures and designed a reconfigurable network architecture and a fault tolerant routing algorithm for a Mesh-of-Tree based Network-on-Chip topology which routes data packets successfully in case of a faults. Also did a brief study and work on the Mapping on the Mesh-of-Tree Network-on-Chip based topology

Pipelined Processor Design

As a part of the course on Computer Architecture, designed a fully pipelined processor on Verilog, which could execute a given sequence of instructions which have been given to be executed

Fault Tolerant Network-on-Chips

Did a study of existing routing algorithms and based on that designed a fault tolerant routing algorithm for a Mesh-of-Tree based Network-on-Chips which routes data packets successfully in case of a fault at any router level

UTI Device

Worked on the design of a device which can track Urine Tract Infection and the device works on the principle of detecting the infection from the color of the sample

Non-invasive Glucometer

Worked on the design of a device which can find glucose level in the blood/saliva using non-invasive methods
