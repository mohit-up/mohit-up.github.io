I am a Senior NPU design engineer at Omnivision Technologies, where I am primarily working on the design of AI accelerator targeting the edge.

I ~~am~~ was a PhD student at the department of Computer Science in the School of Computing at National University of Singapore, where I was a part of the [LSP Group](https://lspgroup.comp.nus.edu.sg/) under [Prof. Li-Shiuan Peh](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~peh/). My research interests revolve around **edge accelerators and system design for neural networks, reconfigurable architectures and on-chip network based accelerators.**

I interned at Xilinx Research Labs Asia Pacific, Singapore (now acquired by AMD) in summer 2020.
Prior to joining NUS, I interned at Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) at Bangalore in the Microprocessor Performance Modelling and Architecture team.

##### **History**

I completed my dual degree Bachelors in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Masters in Physics from [BITS, Pilani](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/) in 2019.
Previously, I was also fortunate enough to work at [HESL Lab](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/hesl) at [NTU, Singapore](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/scse).

My interests vary widely from Computer Science, Electronics to History and Music. I enjoy playing on my guitar from time to time and I am always up for a good badminton game. I like watching historical documentaries and do enjoy working out some times. Feel free to drop by to say hi!!
